Purpose

This website documents academic work on fraud detection in acquiring. It is not an operational product and it does not provide private fraud rules, merchant identities, or deployable decision logic.

Personal data

The website does not intentionally collect personal data. If you contact us by email, your message and address will be used only to respond and manage the academic or professional conversation.

Cookies and analytics

By default, this website does not use tracking cookies. If analytics are enabled in the future, this page will be updated to describe the tool, the purpose, and any opt out options.

Data disclosure policy

No sensitive operational data is published. Public examples rely on synthetic or anonymised datasets. We do not publish merchant identities, personal identifiers, internal fraud rules, or vulnerability enabling details.

  • Synthetic data means artificially generated data intended for demonstration or methodology explanation only.
  • Anonymised data means non reversible aggregation and removal of direct identifiers.

Security and responsible disclosure

If you believe you found a security issue related to this website, please report it responsibly. Do not publish exploit details.
