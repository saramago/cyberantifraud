Purpose

This website documents academic work on fraud detection in acquiring. It is not a product, and it does not provide operational fraud rules or sensitive decision logic.

Personal data

The website does not intentionally collect personal data. If you contact us by email, your message and address will be used solely to respond and manage the collaboration.

Cookies and analytics

By default, this website does not use tracking cookies. If analytics are enabled in the future, this page will be updated to describe the tool, the purpose, and opt out options.

Data disclosure policy

No sensitive operational data is published. Public examples rely on synthetic or anonymised datasets. We do not publish merchant identities, personal identifiers, internal rules, or vulnerability enabling details.

  • Synthetic data means artificially generated data designed for demonstration only.
  • Anonymised data means non reversible aggregation and removal of direct identifiers.

Security and responsible disclosure

If you believe you found a security issue related to this website, please report it responsibly. Do not publish exploit details.
